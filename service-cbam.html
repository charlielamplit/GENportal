What it does

Quarterly EU carbon-border filing, handled end-to-end

The EU's Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) requires importers of carbon-intensive goods to report embedded emissions every quarter. We collect the production data, calculate embedded emissions, prepare the filing, keep up with rule changes, and liaise with verifiers — so your shipments clear customs without delays, penalties or lost orders.

Who needs it

Exporters of CBAM-covered goods to the EU

CBAM currently covers steel, aluminium, cement, fertilizers, hydrogen and electricity. If you export any of these to the EU, you must report embedded emissions every quarter.
