What it does

Know your company's total carbon — and where it hides

We add up your organization's annual greenhouse-gas emissions — direct fuel use, purchased electricity and, if needed, your supply chain — pinpoint the biggest sources, and issue a formal inventory report with reduction advice. The same dataset then powers your ESG report, CBAM filing and green-power planning.

Who needs it

Companies that must report or reduce their carbon

If you are preparing to list, publishing an ESG report, asked by your group or large customers to report emissions, or planning carbon neutrality, you need an organizational inventory first.
