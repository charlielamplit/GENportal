About us

One-stop services for going global

GEN is a flagship initiative under the Shanghai Jien Public Welfare Development Promotion Center. Against the backdrop of a reshaping global economy, we provide enterprises with one-stop internationalization services.

We believe going global is not just expansion — it is safe, sustainable and deep integration into local markets.

Our mission

Help Chinese enterprises view going global not just as expansion, but as safe, sustainable and deep integration — so they don't only "go out," they truly "fit in."

3C Framework

Certify — Comply — Compete

Our work is organized around one integrated framework that covers your entire journey abroad.

01 · Certify

UNGC-aligned ESG training & international certification — your passport to market access and trust.

02 · Comply

Domestic ODI filing & tax incentives, plus on-the-ground overseas legal and regulatory compliance.

03 · Compete

Global resource network, flexible workforce & capability building to win in core markets.
