What it does

Buy the right amount of green power — the smart way

Green power and Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs) are the official proof that your electricity came from wind, solar and other renewables. We size how much you need, procure the certificates or connect you to a green-power exchange, organize the purchase records, and — combined with your carbon inventory and CBAM position — show you the most cost-effective way to buy.

Who needs it

Companies decarbonizing or asked to use green power

If you want to cut emissions, are committing to RE100, are required to use green power for exports, or have done CBAM and want to offset costs, this is for you.
