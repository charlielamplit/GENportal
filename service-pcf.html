What it does

Know exactly how much carbon one product emits

We add up the emissions of a single product across its whole life — raw materials, inbound logistics, manufacturing, distribution, use and end-of-life — issue a formal report, and can produce a carbon label you can put on the product or share with buyers.

Who needs it

Exporters and suppliers asked for product carbon data

If you export products, or supply large brands and factories that require product emissions data or a carbon label, this is for you.
